Not sure what to build? Here are some project ideas to inspire you. Remember, the best projects solve real problems — but don't limit yourself to these suggestions!

<Aside type="tip">
  The best hackathon projects often come from personal pain points. What problem do YOU face that software could solve?
</Aside>

## What Makes a Winning Project?

### Innovation (25% of score)
- Solve a problem in a new way
- Combine technologies creatively
- Think outside the box

### Execution (25% of score)
- Clean, working code
- Good user experience
- Thoughtful architecture

### AI Utilization (20% of score)
- Show creative use of AI in development
- Document your AI-assisted workflow
- Demonstrate the Vibe Coding approach

### Impact (15% of score)
- Real-world applicability
- Potential user base
- Meaningful problem-solving

### Presentation (15% of score)
- Clear demo video
- Well-documented README
- Engaging Vibe Sessions
